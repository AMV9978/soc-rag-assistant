# SOC Triage Framework

## Minimum steps
1) Validate (false positive vs true positive)
2) Identify entity (host/user/app)
3) Collect evidence (logs/process/network/auth)
4) Scope (how many systems/users?)
5) Contain if risk of spread
6) Escalate per severity
7) Document timeline

## Evidence checklist
- Who / What / Where / When / How
- IOCs: hashes, domains, IPs, filenames
