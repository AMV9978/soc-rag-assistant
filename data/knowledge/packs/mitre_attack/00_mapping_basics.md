# MITRE ATT&CK Mapping Basics

## Tactic vs Technique
- Tactic = adversary goal (e.g., Initial Access)
- Technique = how (e.g., Phishing T1566)

## Why it helps
- Common language, coverage tracking, reporting

## Examples
- Phishing: T1566 (Initial Access)
- Remote Services: T1021 (Lateral Movement)
- Credential Dumping: T1003 (Credential Access)
