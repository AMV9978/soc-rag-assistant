# Logging Basics

## Why logging matters
Logs are evidence for detection, investigations, and compliance.

## What to log
- Authentication events (success/fail, MFA, resets)
- Admin actions (group changes, policy edits)
- Endpoint activity (process creation, PowerShell)
- Network/DNS/proxy traffic
- Cloud control plane logs (AWS CloudTrail)

## Good hygiene
- Centralize in SIEM
- Normalize fields
- Time sync (NTP)
- Retention policy
